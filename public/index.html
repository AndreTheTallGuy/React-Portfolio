About Me

As a lifelong learner, I have consistently pushed myself to explore and discover new things. This drive has taken me to some amazing places such as working at an elephant sanctuary in Thailand, teaching English in France and Chile, and even working as a raptor handler. In addition to learning languages and libraries, I've developed the skills to modify existing software, debug code, and adapt to new technologies. I am now enthusiastically searching for my first job as a junior developer where I can utilize all of these skills and learn some new ones as well.
